Tamagotchi

Little flashback
to '90s!

How to Play: Please name your Tamagotchi to start.The point of the game is to keep your tamagotchi satisfied by preventing him/her from hunger, sleepiness and boredom levels reaching 0. Use the buttons on the screen to satify your tamagotchi as the alerts pop up. If you reach 0, you die and the game stops. If you manage to keep your tamagotchi satisfied long enough, you will see him/her morph! Good luck!
